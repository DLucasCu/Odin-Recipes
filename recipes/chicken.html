Juicy Baked Chicken Recipe

This quick and easy recipe will sure help everyone trying to eat a bit better. Personally, I love a great chicken and rice combo as it's super cost effective and keeps me full for the night. It's very easy to make extremely dry chicken and I'm afraid it took me a long time to come ujp with this recipe, yet all of the hassle was worth it.

Ingredients Needed

  • Brown Sugar
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt & Pepper
*Now I only have made a recipe guide for the chicken and the directions on making it, however add anything you wish to help compliment this recipe. Potato salad, rice, and sweet potatoes have proven well as a secondary dish.*

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 F
  2. Depending on size of chicken breasts cut them down, then use meat mallet.
  3. Season as you wish. (I don't believe in measuring seasonings)
  4. Grab baking sheet and spray with Pam or alternative and place parchment paper.
  5. Once oven is ready place in oven for 18-20 minutes, once surface is caramelized its cooked perfectly.
